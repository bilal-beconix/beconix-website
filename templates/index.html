About AI Phone Agents

What are AI Phone Agents? ▼

AI Phone Agents are intelligent phone systems that can have natural, human-like conversations with your patients. They can answer questions, book appointments, qualify enquiries, and transfer calls to your team, all without sounding robotic or scripted.

How realistic do they actually sound? ▼

Very. Our agents use the latest voice AI technology to deliver natural-sounding conversations with real-time responses. They handle interruptions, pauses, and complex back-and-forth dialogue just like a trained receptionist would. We'll demo a live call during your discovery session so you can hear it firsthand.

Can they handle complex conversations, or just simple scripts? ▼

Our agents go well beyond basic scripts. They can handle objections, ask follow-up questions, navigate multi-step conversations, and adapt based on what the caller says. We build custom conversation flows tailored to your specific practice scenarios.

Can the agent handle both inbound and outbound? ▼

Yes. Inbound agents answer and route incoming calls, book appointments, and handle enquiries. Outbound agents can follow up with patients, send appointment reminders, and run recall campaigns, all at scale.

Pricing & Ownership

How much does it cost to build? ▼

Custom builds start from US$599 as a one-time investment. We engineer your AI Phone Agent around your exact workflows, integrations, and patient journeys, then hand it over. You own it outright.

What is Managed AI? ▼

Managed AI is our ongoing service that keeps your agent performing after launch. It includes monitoring, maintenance, optimisation, and updates as your practice evolves. Every build includes 60 days of Managed AI included, then continues month-to-month with no lock-in. Cancel anytime.

What does Managed AI include? ▼

Four things. Monitoring: we watch your agent's performance and catch failures before you notice them. Maintenance: API updates, platform changes, and automation fixes handled by our team. Optimisation: monthly call reviews where we improve responses based on real patient interactions. Adaptation: new staff, new location, new service? We update the agent as your practice evolves.

What are the ongoing platform costs? ▼

Platform costs are passed through at cost with zero markup. Voice AI runs $0.14–$0.20/min and phone numbers cost $0.01–$0.02/min. No hidden fees.

Is this a subscription, or do I own the system? ▼

You own it outright. The custom build is a one-time investment with no lock-in contracts. Managed AI is optional and month-to-month, so you're never locked in.

What's the ROI compared to hiring staff? ▼

A single receptionist costs $50,000–$70,000/year and can only handle one call at a time. Your AI Phone Agent handles unlimited simultaneous calls around $0.10/min, works 24/7, and never calls in sick. Most practices see the ROI within the first few months from reduced after-hours staffing costs alone.

Integration & Technical

Can it integrate with my existing systems? ▼

Yes. We've built integrations with Cliniko, Jane App, HubSpot, Zoho CRM, Calendly, Google Calendar, Spruce Health, Hotdoc, Medirecords, Acuity, Mindbody, Salesforce, GoHighLevel, and many others. If your system has an API, we can connect it.

Can it book appointments directly on my calendar? ▼

Absolutely. Calendar booking is one of the most popular features. The agent checks your availability in real time and books appointments directly, no double-ups, no manual entry.

Can the agent transfer calls to a live person? ▼

Yes. You can set rules for when the agent should transfer. For example, urgent clinical questions, high-value enquiries, or specific request types get routed to your team in real time.

Can it send texts or emails after a call? ▼

Yes. We can set up automated follow-ups like SMS confirmations, email summaries, or CRM updates triggered immediately after each call.

Implementation

How long does it take to build? ▼

Typically 2 to 3 weeks. We handle everything from mapping your call flows to building, testing, and deploying your agent. You don't need to configure anything yourself.

What do you need from us to get started? ▼

We start with a free discovery call to map your exact call flows, patient types, edge cases, and existing tools. From there, we just need access to your systems and any existing scripts or FAQs you want the agent to reference.

What does the testing process look like? ▼

We run live testing with real patient scenarios before you go live. Every edge case is covered. You'll get to make test calls yourself, provide feedback, and we'll refine until it meets your standards.

Compliance & Security

Is it HIPAA compliant? ▼

Yes. HIPAA compliance isn't a footnote for us, it's our foundation. We use HIPAA-compliant integration layers (including Keragon) to handle PHI safely. Patient data is never used to train models, and conversations are processed, not retained for AI learning.

Does the agent handle clinical decisions? ▼

No. AI handles admin, not clinical decisions. Any sensitive or clinical question is immediately routed to a qualified human through built-in escalation paths.

Does the agent need to disclose that it's AI? ▼

Regulations vary by region, but we always recommend transparency. We can configure the agent to introduce itself as an AI assistant, which we've found actually builds trust with callers.

Can it handle high call volumes? ▼

Yes. The system can handle unlimited simultaneous calls, so you'll never miss a patient during peak periods. Something that's impossible with a single receptionist.

Can it scale across multiple locations or use cases? ▼

Absolutely. Once the core agent is built, we can duplicate and customise it for different locations, departments, or use cases without starting from scratch.

What types of practices do you work with? ▼

We've built agents for dental practices, physiotherapy clinics, concierge medicine, allied health, home health, and multi-location networks. Each specialty has different workflows, different edge cases, and different tools. We handle all of it.
